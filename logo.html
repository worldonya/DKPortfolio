Logotypes

My logos are created with a focus on simplicity and clarity. I strive to capture the essence of the brand through minimalist design and smart use of shape and color. Each logo is designed to be easily recognizable and work effectively across various contexts, from digital to print.

This logo was designed for "Lour Concept Store," inspired by the name "Lour," which refers to a type of tree. The design creatively incorporates the word "Lour" into the shape of a tree, symbolizing growth, nature, and elegance. The circular frame adds balance and cohesion, while the sleek black-and-white palette emphasizes simplicity and sophistication, perfectly aligning with the concept store's unique and modern identity.

This logo was designed for a children's clothing store. The playful design uses bold, colorful letters with a soft, rounded shape to create a fun and approachable feel. The "O" forms a cute bear face, adding a whimsical and child-friendly element, perfectly aligning with the store's focus on kids. The vibrant palette and simple layout make the logo eye-catching and memorable.

This logo was created for a gold shop named "Be Unique." The design features elegant, flowing typography that conveys sophistication and exclusivity. The golden hue reinforces the connection to luxury and precious metals, while the emblem at the top incorporates the letter "B" in a diamond-like shape, symbolizing value and uniqueness. The overall design is sleek and refined, perfectly representing the shop's commitment to high-quality and distinct products.

This logo was designed for a flower shop named "Shamim Spring." The design features an elegant handwritten script to convey a sense of softness and natural beauty. At the center, a minimalist rose illustration symbolizes growth, elegance, and the essence of spring. The black-and-white palette ensures timelessness and versatility, perfectly reflecting the shop's connection to nature and refined aesthetic.

This logo was created for a Malaysian business specializing in resin and hotmelt adhesives. The crescent moon, inspired by the Malaysian flag, symbolizes national identity and trust. The warm yellow typography stands out against the deep blue background, creating a professional and reliable image that reflects the brand's high-quality products.

This logo for SötBi is a blend of the bee's energy and the saffron flower's elegance, created with simplicity and style. Its minimalist design reflects SötBi's focus on pure, high-quality products, while the golden tones convey luxury and excellence. This iconic design highlights a brand that values nature and exclusivity.
