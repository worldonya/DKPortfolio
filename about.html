About me

Creative Designer with Versatility and Passion

Hi, I’m Donya, a passionate digital designer and a student of Digital Design at Kristianstad University. I thrive on creativity and attention to detail, aiming to merge functionality and aesthetics in every project I take on. I find inspiration in the world around me, transforming ideas into unique visual solutions. Beyond design, I enjoy sketching, exploring artistic expressions, and learning new tools to expand my skills. I love cats, food, watching movies and series (often at the same time!), driving, and creating with any paper or materials I can get my hands on. Whether it's crafting logos, building user-friendly websites, or creating prototypes, my goal is to deliver designs that leave a lasting impression. My work is driven by a commitment to innovation, quality, and a touch of personal flair.
