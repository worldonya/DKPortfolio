Business Card

I design business cards that are clear and eye-catching. With smart layouts, colors, and fonts, I create designs that make an impact and reflect the brand's identity.

This business card features a bold and modern design with abstract shapes and a harmonious color palette of blue, beige, and pink. The flowing shapes are inspired by the Persian script of my name, creating a visually dynamic and deeply personal connection to my cultural identity. The name and title are placed centrally in a thick, modern font to balance the organic shapes and ensure clarity. The design merges creativity, individuality, and professionalism, perfectly reflecting the essence of a digital designer.

This business card features a minimalist and elegant design with a personal touch. The right side showcases a self-portrait sketch created by me, adding an artistic and unique element that reflects my creativity and individuality. The name is highlighted in a modern script font for a stylish and approachable feel, while the contact details are presented in a clean, sans-serif font to ensure readability. The monochrome color scheme emphasizes professionalism and timelessness, making the design both personal and impactful.

This business card embraces a bold, minimalist aesthetic with a strong focus on typography. The name is displayed in large, block-style letters that dominate the design, creating a striking and confident impression. The vertical placement of the job title adds a unique and dynamic touch, breaking away from traditional layouts while maintaining clarity and readability. The black-and-white color scheme reinforces a timeless and professional look, ensuring the design is both impactful and versatile. This card reflects a bold and straightforward approach, perfectly suited for a digital designer who values clarity and creativity.
